About Us – Welcome to Unblocked Games

Unblocked Games is brought to you by XYZ Games LLC, a passionate team of developers, designers, and storytellers dedicated to creating immersive mobile gaming experiences. Launched in 2020, our flagship title Unblocked Games has already delighted millions of players worldwide with its fast‑paced mountain racing, stunning graphics, and uniquely customizable snowboards.

Our Mission

We believe that gaming should be an escape—a place to challenge yourself, explore breathtaking landscapes, and connect with friends. Our mission is to deliver high‑quality, easy‑to‑pick‑up but hard‑to‑master games that reward skill, creativity, and community engagement.

Why Trust Us?

  • We do not collect personal data
  • We serve zero ads or popups
  • We don’t use third-party tracking scripts
  • Our game is COPPA-aligned, safe for students and kids
Your privacy and focus come first — always.

What Makes Unblocked Games Special

  • Dynamic Environments: Race through snowy peaks, frozen forests, and hidden ice caves—each level is hand‑crafted to deliver fresh thrills.

  • Deep Customization: Unlock and upgrade over 50 snowboards, each with unique abilities and designs. Mix and match gear, outfits, and decals to define your style.

  • Competitive Spirit: Climb the global leaderboards, compete in weekly tournaments, and challenge friends head‑to‑head in real‑time races.

  • Ongoing Support: We regularly release new events, tracks, and seasonal content to keep gameplay exciting and unexpected.

Who Is This For?

  • Students on school devices
  • Casual gamers looking for quick fun
  • Parents seeking safe games for their children
  • Anyone who enjoys the challenge of dodging obstacles!
